ANIMALS MAP PLACES

MONKEY TRAIL

The Monkey Trail is a thrilling walkway that lets you explore the lively world of primates. Watch playful monkeys swing through the trees and interact with their surroundings.

Featured species:

  • Vervet Monkeys
  • Capuchins
  • Spider Monkeys

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The Monkey Trail is near the eastern side of the zoo, adjacent to the Lost Forest.
